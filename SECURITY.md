## Response Timeline

We aim to follow this response process:

- **Acknowledgement:** within 48 hours
- **Initial assessment:** within 5 days
- **Fix or mitigation:** as soon as reasonably possible
- **Public disclosure:** after a fix has been released

Timelines may vary depending on severity and complexity.
